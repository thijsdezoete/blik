The Core Question

Do you need an ERP? If you're managing business operations beyond HR (sales, inventory, accounting), Odoo's integrated approach makes sense. The HR module is basic but functional.

Do you just need great performance reviews? Blik gives you enterprise-grade 360¬∞ feedback without the complexity of a full ERP. Deploy in 5 minutes, integrate via API.

Deployment & Operational Complexity

Blik: Simple by Design

  • Install: docker-compose up (5 min)
  • Requirements: Docker only
  • Configuration: Environment variables
  • Maintenance: Automated updates
  • Team training: < 1 hour
  • Resource usage: Minimal (< 1GB RAM)

Odoo: Enterprise Complexity

  • Install: Multi-step setup (hours to days)
  • Requirements: PostgreSQL, Python, nginx
  • Configuration: Module dependencies, permissions
  • Maintenance: Complex update process
  • Team training: Days to weeks
  • Resource usage: Heavy (2GB+ RAM typical)
Reality Check: Odoo is powerful, but that power comes with complexity. Many companies underestimate the effort required for deployment, customization, and user training. Consider whether you need that complexity for performance reviews alone.
