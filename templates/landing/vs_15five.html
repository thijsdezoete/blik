The Engagement Trap

Continuous feedback sounds appealing—until questions become predictable. Research shows survey fatigue sets in when respondents can predict what you're going to ask. 15Five's time-triggered surveys from a fixed library create exactly this problem. By month 7-8, employees are clicking through without thinking. Blik takes a different approach: deeper, less frequent reviews with fresh questions that adapt to your organization's evolving needs. Quality over quantity.
