When a user reports "the system crashing and user losing all the stored data - this has happened to me twice," that's not a minor inconvenience. Both Open Source, Different Quality Standards

OrangeHRM and Blik are both open source. But being open source doesn't determine quality - architecture and execution do. OrangeHRM is a comprehensive HR suite that's been around since 2005, carrying two decades of legacy decisions. Blik is purpose-built for 360° feedback with modern infrastructure, clean UX, and production-grade stability. Both are free to self-host, but one requires begging support to add a leave type while the other gives you full admin control. One crashes and loses data, the other runs on battle-tested Django. Free doesn't have to feel cheap.
